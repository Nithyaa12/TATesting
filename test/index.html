Test Structure

The written test of Team Antariksh features question types that closely reflect the kind of thinking you'll do in a student space technology team. You are automatically registered for the test if you have registered online through the registration form.

There will be 2 sections in the written test:

  • Aptitude Section
  • Technical Section


The Aptitude Section: This section will consist of 15 questions, directly testing your logical reasoning abilities. There is no prerequisite for this section, but the next section will definitely require a revision of concepts.

The Technical Section: This section will consist of a total of 5 questions, selected at random from a pool of topics mentioned below :

- Mechanics
- Law of Gravitation
- Rotational Dynamics
- Rigid Body and Elasticity
- Kinematics
- Thermodynamics
- Wave Propogation
- Digital Electronics
- Magnetism
- Fluid Mechanics
- Optics

Note: There will also be 1 situation based question given at the end of the test, which has to be answered mandatorily.

The test taker needs to know about all the topics before appearing for the test. An email has already been sent to you indicating the time and date of the test. For further information, please contact any of our team representatives by navigating to the contact section.
